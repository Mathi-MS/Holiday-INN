Express & Suites

Pineville - Alexandria

Holiday Inn Express & Suites in Pineville offers a unique combination of small-town charm and city convenience. As a smoke-free hotel, it is situated in a growing business district, close to significant companies and medical facilities. This location suits both business and leisure travelers.

Located just 4 miles from the Riverfront Convention Center, our hotel provides easy access to local attractions including sports complexes, lakes, and the Alexandria Museum of Art. It is also conveniently located near educational institutions like the Louisiana State University, Louisiana Christian University, and the Pineville Beauty School.
